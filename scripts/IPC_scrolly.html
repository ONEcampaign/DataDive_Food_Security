Phase 2 - stressed

Households have minimal food consumption but are unable to afford some essential non-food expenditures.

In 2022 this affects:

212 million people
in 39 countries.

Phase 3 - crisis

Households either have acute malnutrition because they done have enough food or can only afford it by selling essential assets.

In 2022 this affects:

111 million people
in 39 countries.

Phase 4 - emergency

Households have very high acute malnutrition,some family members die. They can only eat by selling all their assets.

In 2022 this affects:

30 million people
in 33 countries.

Phase 5 - catastrophe

Starvation, death, destitution and extreme acute malnutrition.

In 2022 this affects:

219 thousand people
in 4 countries.
